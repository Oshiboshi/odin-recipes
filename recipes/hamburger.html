Hamburger

Worlds best hamburger

This recipe is aflavor sensation!

Done in 10 minutes, quick and easy.

Ingredients
  • 500g - Meat
  • 1pc - Onions
  • ipc - egg
  • salt
  • Black pepper
  • 4pc - Burger paddies
  1. Finely chop the onion.
  2. Mix meat with te rest of the ingredients.
  3. Make four equal meat paddies.
  4. Frye on medium high for 3-4min per side.
  5. Putt meat in the paddies.
