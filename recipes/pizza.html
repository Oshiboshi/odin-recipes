Pizza

Authentic pizza

Done in 40 minutes.

Ingredients
  • 1pc - pizza dough
  • 170g mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2tsp - Oregano
  • 1can - tomato sauce
  1. Preheat the oven to 288 degrees C.
  2. Spreed tomato sauce evenly on the entire pizza.
  3. Add even layer of cheese.
  4. Bake in lower part or te oven for 4-6 min.
