Lasagna

Worlds best lasagna

This recipe will knock there socks of!

Done in 35 minutes in just a few simple steps!

Ingredients
  • 500g - Meat
  • Lasagna noodle cheats
  • 1pc - Onions
  • 2pc - Garlic cloves
  • 1cans - Crushed tomatoes
  • 2cans - tomato sauce
  • 2tbs - sugar
  • 150g - parmesan cheese
  • 200g - Mozzarella cheese
  • salt
  • Black pepper
  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the cheese mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
